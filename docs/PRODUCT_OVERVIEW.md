# Product Overview

AI Acquisitions OS is an AI-assisted real estate acquisitions CRM for managing sellers, conversations, offers, property analysis, dispositions, transactions, documents, team setup, and SaaS-ready settings.

## Core Product Areas

- Pipeline Board: tracks leads and deal stages.
- Seller Workspace: central workspace for seller context, tasks, intelligence, follow-up, offers, transaction planning, and communications.
- Conversation Hub: unified seller interaction timeline with SMS today and future-ready channels.
- SMS System: Twilio-backed inbound/outbound SMS through Netlify Functions.
- AI Intelligence Dashboard: rule-based intelligence summary for deal quality, urgency, risks, and next action.
- AI Copilot: OpenAI-backed, rule-fallback acquisition assistant with contextual chat.
- Workflow Engine: rule-based workflow recommendations and approval foundation.
- Offer System: internal offer readiness, range, strategy, builder, and scenario comparison.
- Property Intelligence: manual comps, ARV, repairs, rent potential, and exit strategy analysis.
- Buyer CRM: manual buyer matching and dispositions planning.
- Transaction Management: internal tracking for contract, title, buyer assignment, and closing readiness.
- Documents / Contract Prep: internal document readiness and contract preparation checklist.
- Team / Roles: foundation for roles, assignments, and permission visibility.
- Settings / Organization: foundation for organization profile, markets, lead sources, and system preferences.

## Current Boundaries

The platform is not yet a full multi-tenant SaaS. Team, settings, documents, automations, and many planning modules are local/manual foundations until persistence and enforcement schemas are approved.

AI guidance is internal and review-only. It does not automatically send messages, create offers, generate legal documents, or execute workflows.
